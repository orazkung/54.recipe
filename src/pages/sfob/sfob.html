Stir fried onion blossom with pork

Ingredient

  • 200 g thinly sliced pork neck
  • 250 g onion blossom cut to 1 inch size
  • 100 g thinly sliced carrot
  • 100 g halved shitake mushroom
  • 2 tbs. minced garlic
  • 2 tbs. vegetable oil
  • ½ tsp. sugar
  • 1 tbs. fish sauce
  • 1 tbs. oyster sauce

Direction

  1. Preheat and grease the wok with 2 tbs. of vegetable oil, add minced garlic to stir fry over medium – high heat. Add thinly sliced pork neck to stir fry until cooked.
  2. Add carrot, onion blossom and fresh shitake mushroom.
  3. Season with sugar, fish sauce and oyster sauce and stir fry until cooperated before turning off the heat.
  4. Ready to serve.
