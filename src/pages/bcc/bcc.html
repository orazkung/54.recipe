Butter Chocolate Cupcake

Ingredient

  • 300 g cake flour
  • 20 g baking powder
  • ½ tsp. salt
  • ½ tsp. vanilla powder
  • 250 g sugar
  • 15 g SP
  • 7 eggs
  • 120 g fresh milk
  • 180 g melted butter
  • 3 tbs. cocoa powder
  • 3 tbs. vegetable oil

Direction

  1. Preheat oven to 180 C. Line standard muffin tins with paper liners.
  2. Add baking powder and vanilla powder to cake flour and shift once.
  3. Add all ingredients except melt butter (cake flour, baking powder, vanilla powder, salt, sugar, SP, eggs and fresh milk in mixing bowl, beat with the lowest speed for 1 minute.
  4. Turn the speed up to the highest and continue to beat for 5 minutes.
  5. Turn the speed down to the lowest again, gently and gradually pour in melted butter into the mixing bowl (like a tiny string) and continue to mix with the lowest speed for 1 minute.
  6. Mix well cocoa powder and vegetable oil and add to mixing bowl and mix to just combine.
  7. Divide the batter evenly among lined cup, filling each three – quarters full. Bake rotating tins halfway through, until a cake tester inserted in centers come out clean, about 15-20 minutes. Transfer to wire racks to cool completely.
  8. Ready to serve.
