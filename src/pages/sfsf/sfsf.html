Stir friend season fish

Ingredient

  • 250 g seasoned white bait fish
  • 80 g Phad prik khing or red curry paste
  • 7-8 kaffir lime leaves, julienned
  • 1 tbs. sugar
  • 1-2 tbs. fish sauce

Direction

  1. Preheat and lightly grease the wok with vegetable oil, add red curry paste to stir fry over medium â€“ low heat until aroma and add some of julienned kaffir lime leaves.
  2. Season with sugar and fish sauce and mix well.
  3. Add seasoned white baits to mix until incorporate. Sprinkle with the remaining kaffir lime leaves.
  4. Ready to serve.
