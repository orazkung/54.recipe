Mac and cheese

Ingredient

  • 300g dried macaroni pasta
  • 40g butter
  • 2 tablespoons plain flour
  • 1 cup milk
  • 1 cup thickened cream
  • 1 cup grated vintage cheddar
  • 1/2 cup grated gruy√®re
  • 50g cream cheese, chopped
  • 2 teaspoons American mustard

Direction

  1. Cook pasta following packet directions. Drain. Return to pan.
  2. Meanwhile, melt butter in a saucepan over medium-high heat. Add flour. Cook, stirring, for 1 minute or until bubbling. Gradually add milk and cream, stirring until smooth and combined. Cook, stirring, for 4 to 5 minutes until mixture boils and thickens. Add cheeses and mustard. Stir until melted and mixture is smooth.
  3. Add cheese mixture to pasta. Stir to combine. Stand for 5 minutes. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Ready to serve.
